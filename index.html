ABOUT ME --------

My name is Kyle Marvin T. Buenaventura. I discovered my love for basketball when I was 9, and since then, it has been more than just a sport to me—it’s my way of staying grounded. Basketball brings me happiness, helps me manage stress, and serves as a form of escape where I can forget about my worries for a while. The thrill of playing, whether it’s a friendly game or a competitive match, keeps me motivated and energized.

Beyond basketball, I’m passionate about self-improvement and personal growth. I believe in staying active, not just physically but also mentally, as I’m always eager to learn new things and face challenges. Whether it’s exploring new hobbies, spending quality time with friends and family, or pushing myself to improve on and off the court, I strive to make the most of each day and stay positive even in difficult situations.
